# AI

LinkOps will support AI capabilities through explicit boundaries for agents, prompts, retrieval, graph intelligence, and orchestration.

LinkOps is designed as a future-ready platform capable of supporting asset intelligence, knowledge graph exploration, AI copilot workflows, root cause analysis, compliance intelligence, industrial IoT, live monitoring, and digital twin integration.

## Planned AI Areas

- Multi-agent workflows
- Prompt management
- Retrieval augmented generation
- Knowledge graph reasoning
- Document intelligence
- Asset intelligence
- Industrial copilot experiences

## Principles

- Keep AI orchestration separate from core domain rules.
- Treat prompts and retrieval flows as versioned platform assets.
- Preserve traceability for generated outputs.
- Design for observability, evaluation, and governance from the beginning.

No AI, LLM, agent, RAG, or prompt logic is implemented in this skeleton.
