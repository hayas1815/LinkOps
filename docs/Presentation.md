# Presentation

LinkOps

Industrial Knowledge Intelligence Platform

Connect. Understand. Operate.

## Executive Summary

LinkOps transforms fragmented industrial knowledge into a unified operational intelligence platform by connecting documents, assets, maintenance history, engineering knowledge, compliance records, and future real-time sensor data into a single AI-powered system.

## Platform Pillars

- Asset intelligence
- Industrial document intelligence
- Knowledge graph intelligence
- AI copilots and multi-agent workflows
- IoT and SCADA integration
- Live monitoring and analytics
- Enterprise governance and deployment readiness

## Current Deliverable

This repository provides the production-ready skeleton for future platform development. It defines boundaries, documentation, placeholders, and deployment structure without implementing business logic.
